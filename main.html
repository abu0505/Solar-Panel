Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about solar installation and benefits.

Yes, solar panels still generate power on cloudy or rainy days using diffused sunlight. While energy production is reduced compared to sunny days (typically 10-25% of peak capacity), modern panels are highly efficient and continue working. Plus, rain naturally cleans your panels, maintaining optimal performance.
Standard grid-tied systems automatically shut down during outages for safety reasons. However, if you add a battery backup system, you'll have power during outages. Our battery solutions can keep essential appliances running for hours or even days, depending on your usage and battery capacity.
All our installations come with a comprehensive 25-year performance warranty guaranteeing at least 85% efficiency after 25 years. Equipment manufacturers provide 10-15 year product warranties, and we offer a 5-year workmanship guarantee on installation. You're fully protected for decades.
Most residential installations take 1-3 days, depending on system size and roof complexity. Commercial projects may take longer. The timeline includes: site assessment (1-2 weeks), permitting (2-6 weeks), installation (1-3 days), and grid connection approval (1-2 weeks). We handle all paperwork and coordination.
